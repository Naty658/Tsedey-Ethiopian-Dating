Tsedey Child Safety Standards

Tsedey strictly prohibits any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation. All users must be 18 years of age or older.

Any content involving minors is immediately removed, and reports are sent to the appropriate authorities.

Users can report unsafe or suspicious content through the in-app report feature or by emailing support@tsedeyapp.web.app.

Tsedey complies with all relevant child safety and online protection laws.
